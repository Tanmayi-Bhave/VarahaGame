Game Rules

  1. It is a two player game
  2. You have to score 50 to win
  3. A person who scores 50 first wins the game
  4. You can roll a dice and the score will get added
  5. If you get 1 on the dice, the dice sum will be zero
  6. If you click on hold, you can hold the current total dice score and it will get added to your actual player score.
  7. Once a player wins the game, you won't be able to play. To play again, you need to click on the new game button.
  8. When you click on New game, the game will restart.
